Wonder in The Woods

Lost Code of Gaia is a relaxing and unpressuring game which its main goals is for players to learn about rare trees. Join this journey through the woods and save forests together!

About the Game

Story

You as Nova is the only person in your bloodline who was given the mission to save polluted forests. You are given a Journal from your great-grandmother, Dr. Gaia Lin. Follow the Journal clues to success.

Gameplay

Use arrow keys or WASD to move the player. Click at trees in Level 1 to read and memorise the facts. In Level 2, read the recipe before collecting potions, drag selected potions into the pot and click submit to win.

Graphics

Pixellated, 2D graphics, free-form map (Using PicsArt).

AI Feature

AI detection element added in this game in Level 1. This AI detects clickable objects atound the player and will notify player about it.
